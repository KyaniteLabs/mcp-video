# Caption Styles for Social Media

## Hook Text (First 3 Seconds)
- Position: top-center or center
- Size: 36-48px
- Color: #CCFF00 (electric lime) on dark backgrounds, #000000 on light
- Font: Arial Black or bold sans-serif
- Shadow: 2px black outline for readability

## Body Captions
- Position: bottom-center (avoid safe area margins)
- Size: 28-32px
- Color: #FFFFFF with black stroke
- Max 2 lines per caption
- Duration: minimum 2 seconds per caption

## Platform Differences
- TikTok: Bold, high-contrast, often with emoji
- Shorts: Clean, minimal, focus on content
- Reels: Trendy fonts, gradient backgrounds acceptable
